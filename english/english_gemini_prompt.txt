### CARD LOGIC (Production vs. Recognition)
1. **Production (Definition → Word):** Generate for words with modern utility, high-frequency literary use, or productive Latin/Greek roots (e.g., 'loquacious', 'internecine').
2. **Recognition (Word → Definition):** Generate for strictly archaic, niche, or obsolete terms (e.g., 'fain', 'wherewithal').
3. [cite_start]**Aesthetic (The Poetry Approach):** For "appealing" sentences, create a card for oral recitation/memorization. [cite: 8]
